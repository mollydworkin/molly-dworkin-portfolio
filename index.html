About Me

Hi! I'm Molly Dworkin. I'm a student at the University of Michigan studying Information Analysis, where I focus on turning data into insights that drive better decisions.
I'm especially interested in how data, technology, and human behavior come together to shape the way we live and interact online.
I love using data to solve real-world problems, whether it's optimizing user experiences or helping organizations make smarter, more informed choices.
I'm excited to keep exploring how analytical thinking and creative problem-solving can work hand-in-hand to make a meaningful impact.
